Our Mission

"Today I will try to meet the expectations of the ones that believed in me and showed me the way. My mom worked and studied to get her Family Nurse Practitioner license while she raised a family. Her parents worked in the furniture business and maintained a working horse farm. These people have been examples of hard work and dedication. Work hard today to make them proud."

Our Goal

Simple. American Dream is alive. To remain in the central NC area, find love, marry, raise children, and establish a respected family owned and operated business.

Business Approach

Not wanting to run a multi-million dollar business. Just a family owned and operated business. Willing to work individually and any small job is a good job for Branson Land Management. Willing to work with property owners for phased approach developing the land over multiple years to spread out the costs.
