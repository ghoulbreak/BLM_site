What types of concrete finishes do you offer?

We offer a variety of concrete finishes including standard broom finish, exposed aggregate, stamped patterns for decorative looks, smooth trowel finish, and colored concrete options. Each finish offers different aesthetic and functional benefits.

How long does it take for a new concrete driveway to cure?

While concrete is typically ready for light foot traffic within 24-48 hours, it takes approximately 7 days to cure enough for vehicle traffic. Full curing takes about 28 days. Weather conditions can affect these timeframes.

What's the difference between tree trimming and tree pruning?

Tree trimming generally focuses on removing overgrown branches for aesthetic purposes, while pruning is more selective and aims to remove specific branches for the tree's health, structure, and fruit production. Both services are important for maintaining healthy, attractive trees.
